Mixed Berry Smoothie

Recipe from Beth @ Budget Bytes: Link

Description

This smoothie recipe is packed with fruit and is a great option for breakfast or as a snack

Ingredients

  • 3 cups frozen mixed berries
  • 1 frozen banana, sliced
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups unsweetened almond milk

Instructions

  1. Add the frozen mixed berries, frozen banana (sliced in half), yogurt, vanilla extract, sugar, and almond milk to a large blender.
  2. Blend the ingredients until smooth. If the smoothie is too thick, add more milk as needed to make it blend smoothly. Serve immediately and enjoy.
